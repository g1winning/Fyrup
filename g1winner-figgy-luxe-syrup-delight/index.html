Why Choose Fyrup?

Fyrup is the ideal natural alternative to refined simple syrup. With its subtle, sophisticated flavor profile, it enhances without overpowering. Unlike empty-calorie refined sugar syrup, Fyrup delivers real nourishment from whole figs – supporting immunity, heart health, and vitality the natural way.

Packed with antioxidants that fight free radicals and oxidative stress

Rich in minerals like potassium, calcium, magnesium, and iron for better blood pressure, bone health, and energy

High in dietary fiber for digestion and gentle blood sugar support (lower glycemic impact than refined sugar)

Natural anti-inflammatory properties

Our figs are grown without any pesticides or herbicides – pure, clean, and straight from nature.

Recipes & Serving Suggestions

Elevate your cocktails, desserts, and everyday drinks with Fyrup's deep, caramel-like fig notes.

Fig Old Fashioned

Fyrup's subtle fig notes complement the bourbon beautifully without overpowering it.

  • • 2 oz bourbon
  • • ½ oz Fyrup
  • • 2 dashes aromatic bitters
  • • Orange twist for garnish

Fig Margarita

The delicate fig flavor adds depth while letting the tequila shine.

  • • 2 oz tequila
  • • 1 oz lime juice
  • • ¾ oz Fyrup
  • • ½ oz triple sec
  • • Salt rim & lime wedge

Breakfast Upgrade

A light drizzle enhances without overwhelming the natural flavors.

  • • Greek yogurt or pancakes
  • • Drizzle of Fyrup
  • • Fresh fruit
  • • Nuts or granola

Fig Mojito

A refreshing twist on the classic with Fyrup's natural sweetness.

  • • 2 oz white rum
  • • 1 oz Fyrup
  • • ¾ oz lime juice
  • • 6-8 mint leaves
  • • Soda water

Fig Glazed Salmon

The perfect balance of sweet and savory for your main course.

  • • 2 salmon fillets
  • • 2 tbsp Fyrup
  • • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • • Garlic powder to taste

Power Smoothie

A nutritious boost with natural sweetness from Fyrup.

  • • 1 banana
  • • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • • 1 tbsp Fyrup
  • • ½ cup almond milk
  • • 1 tbsp chia seeds
